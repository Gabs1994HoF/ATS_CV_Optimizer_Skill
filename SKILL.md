name: ATS Resume & Cover Letter Optimizer
description: Optimizes resumes and generates highly personalized ATS-friendly cover letters from job descriptions.
---

# Purpose

This skill helps candidates tailor their applications to specific job offers.

It:
- analyzes job descriptions
- extracts ATS keywords
- optimizes resumes
- generates natural cover letters
- improves recruiter compatibility

# Workflow

## Step 1 — Analyze Job Description

Use:
- prompts/job_analysis.md

Extract:
- ATS keywords
- technical skills
- soft skills
- hiring priorities
- company culture

---

## Step 2 — Optimize Resume

Use:
- prompts/resume_optimizer.md

Adapt:
- professional summary
- skills
- experience bullet points

Improve:
- ATS compatibility
- readability
- alignment

Never invent fake information.

---

## Step 3 — Generate Cover Letter

Use:
- prompts/cover_letter.md

Generate:
- concise
- natural
- human-sounding
- personalized cover letter

Avoid robotic AI style.

# Inputs

- Resume
- Job description

# Outputs

- ATS analysis
- optimized resume
- personalized cover letter
- suggested keywords
- Commit new file
